Bio

Hi! I'm Harry Dresden.

I'm a Licensed Private Investigator operating in the Greater Chicagoland area.
I hear that the internet is like the Yellow Pages these days. I can't use a computer, so I asked some friends of mine to se up this website for me.

Experience:

  • Practicioner of Magic since age 10.
  • Ragged Angel Investigations - Associate P.I.
  • Harry Dresden, Wizard - Private Investigator.
  • Chicago Police Department - Special Consultant/S.I.D.

My specialties are:

  • Paranormal Investigations.
  • Finding Lost Items.
  • Blowing stuff up.

No, I'm not kidding. No, I don't do parties.

Rates

I charge $50.00 p/hr.
A retainer of $500.00 is customary, but negotiable.
I will send you an itemized bill of everything I do, so you'll have a good idea of what I'm working on.
Free initial consultation. If I feel I can help you best, I will. Otherwise, I can refer you to someone who I think can.
